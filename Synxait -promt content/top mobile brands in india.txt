 Xiaomi makes and invests in smartphones, mobile apps, laptops, home appliances, bags, shoes, consumer electronics, and many other products.Xiaomi is one of India's top smartphone brands because it produces some of the cheapest phones. New product launches by the company in 2023 will be very interesting to see because the last one was very good and successful, especially the MI VR Play 2 which was very innovative. India is a big market for mobile phones and with such a positive response from the public Xiaomi is here to stay for a long time but they will have to bring some new unique innovations to maintain their clientele otherwise any other Chinese or international brand can take over them very easily.
